# BRANDBOOK: THE BLACK PROTOCOL (SAIS) 💎
## Sub-Brand Identification: Sais & Rituais de Banho

---

### 1. THE SOUL OF THE PROJECT
"O Cofre das Botânicas Secretas" is the first movement of the SommersStore. It focuses on the **tactile** and **aromatic** power of minerals and herbs.

### 2. VISUAL ACCENTS (The 30%)
While following the Master Brandbook, the Sais project uses:
- **Primary Image Style**: Darkroom photography, macro texture of salts, high contrast.
- **Specific Palettes**: `#1A1A1A` (Basalt), `#F5F5DC` (Sea Salt White), `#C5A059` (Royal Seal).
- **Icons**: Minimalist botanical sketches.

---

### 3. CONTENT ARCHITECTURE
1. **The Manifesto**: Establishes the ritual.
2. **The Formulas**: Atomic parts (Ingredient + Method + Presentation).
3. **The Vault (Cofre)**: The secure digital area where the user consumes the content.

### 4. PDF FIDELITY SPECS
- **Page Size**: A4 Strict (210x297mm).
- **Layout**: Center-justified typography for manifestos, Grid-based for formulas.
- **Watermark**: "Elite Edition" subtle gold seal on Page 1.
