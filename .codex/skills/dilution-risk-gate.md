# DilutionRiskGate

## Purpose
Protect strong clone bases from weak harmonization.

## Critical Risks
- Generic AI language replacing specific expert language.
- Loss of strong frameworks already present.
- Mixing unrelated domains or another expert's voice.
- Adding weak motivational statements as if they were strategy.
- Rewriting a precise clone into a broad assistant persona.

## Gate Rules
- Any critical identity contamination can force `REJEITAR`.
- High genericity plus low novelty should force `ANEXAR_APENAS`.
- Strong content with messy extraction should force `REVISAR`.
- Only low or controlled risk can become `APROVAR`.

## Output
Return risk level, blocking reasons, mitigation instructions, and final gate status.
